Asian cuisine includes several major regions, Central Asia, East Asia, North Asia, South Asia, Southeast Asia, and West Asia. All regions have very distinct characteristics to their cuisine. Common ingredients among all regions are rice, ginger, garlic, chilies, soy and tofu. Stir frying, steaming and deep frying are common cooking methods. Curry is a popular dish among Asian cultures. Northern India, Pakistan, and Bangladesh use a yogurt base, while Southern India, Sri Lanka and Southeast Asia use coconut milk as a base. Noodles, dumplings, and meats are common dishes.
